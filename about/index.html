Making the digital world a better place, one page at a time.

Amanda Bukur, a Silicon Valley native, took interest in technology and design at a young age. She studied Business Management Information Systems and Art at San Jose State University. In 2014 she graduated with a dual degree and started working as a web content designer. Since then she has expanded her experience in graphic design, social media marketing, and web development.

Amanda spends her weekends either competing in hackathons or surfing the beaches of California.
